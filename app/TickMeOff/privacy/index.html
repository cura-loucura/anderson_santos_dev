TickMeOff — Privacy Policy

TickMeOff stores all your data — areas, projects, tasks, and settings — as plain markdown and JSON files in a vault folder you choose on your own device. The app never sends this data to any developer-managed server. No account is required, and no personal information is collected.

If you choose a vault folder inside iCloud Drive, your files are synced by Apple using iCloud. That sync is governed by Apple's own privacy policy; the developer has no access to your iCloud data at any point.

In-app purchases are handled entirely by the App Store. The developer receives only the anonymised transaction confirmation that Apple provides; no payment details or personal information are shared with the developer.

Pomodoro and reminder notifications are sent locally by the app. No notification content or usage data leaves your device.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact me through the Support page.
